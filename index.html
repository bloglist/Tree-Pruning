Tree Pruning

At Pacific Land Preservation, we understand that a lush, dense canopy may look beautiful but poses risks to your trees' health and safety. Pruning is a critical aspect of tree care, essential not only for maintaining the health and vitality of your trees but also for enhancing their aesthetic appeal. Proper pruning techniques allow wind to flow through the trees, reducing the likelihood of breakage or total collapse during strong winds.

We specialize in pruning a wide range of trees, including conifers, evergreens, deciduous trees, ornamentals, and various fruit trees like apple, cherry, and pear. Our comprehensive pruning service is designed to cater to the specific needs of each tree type, ensuring healthy growth and the preservation of natural beauty. Our pruning approach may involve:

Find Us Here!
  • Raising the Crown:Elevating the lower branches to increase clearance under the tree, making spaces more usable and visually appealing.
  • Correcting Unhealthy Pruning:Addressing and rectifying any damage caused by previous improper pruning practices.
  • Removing Deadwood and Crossing Branches:Eliminating dead or overlapping branches to prevent decay and encourage healthy growth.
  • Reducing the Crown:Strategically lowering the tree's height without resorting to harmful topping, ensuring the tree's structural integrity and health.
  • Thinning for Light and Air Flow:Carefully thinning the canopy to enhance light penetration and air circulation, vital for the tree's health and the surrounding environment.

With Pacific Land Preservation, you're choosing a partner committed to the art and science of tree pruning, dedicated to the longevity, health, and beauty of your trees.
